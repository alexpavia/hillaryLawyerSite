About Hillary

Experience

Hillary Pavia is an associate at Tycksen & Shattuck in Draper, UT in the Litigation group and advises clients in a variety of litigation matters. Her experience includes drafting motions and case briefs and conducting research. Prior to joining the firm as an attorney, Hillary was a law clerk with Tycksen & Shattuck while attending law school at the University of Utah (2012-2014).

Prior to law school, Hillary was an education intern with the Federal Judicial Center in Washington, DC (2011). She assisted in publishing the Interactive Bankruptcy Online Tutorial (iBOT) for court staff bankruptcy law training and certification and researched, wrote and edited scripts for “Court to Court TV”, A Moment in Court History segments.
